About us

WHAT DO WE DO?

Ozone Gym Has Been The Authority In Fitness Since 1965 Dating Back To The Original Ozone Gym In Venice, California. It Was The Place For Serious Fitness. Opened Long Before The Modern Day Health Club Existed, The Original Ozone Gym Featured Homemade Equipment And A Dedication To Getting Results.


The Year 2019 Marked The 17th Anniversary Of OZone Gym India. In These Glorious 17 Years Ozone Gym Has Cut Out For Itself more than 20 Gyms In India Across 12+ Cities And A Few More Ready To Start In The Near Future.



WHY ARE WE DIFFERENT?

Ozone Gym Has Multiple Awards For Excellence Under Its Belt. Our Gym Has Expanded Its Fitness Profile To Offer All Of The Latest Equipment And Services Making It The Best In The Industry. Our Gym Believes That Fitness Is Not Just About A Toned Body But The Power To Overcome All The Obstacles.



WHY CHOOSE US?

Known For The Unrivalled Success Of Providing State-Of-The-Art Equipment And Fitness Knowledge With A Globally Proven Fitness Training Module And With Certified Trainers And Nutritional Counselling We Have Had A Comprehensive Approach To The Overall Health And Wellbeing Of The Members.
