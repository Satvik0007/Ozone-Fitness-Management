VIRTUAL TRAINING

Virtual training refers to training done in a simulated environment. Train from the comfort of your home with our guides below.

1. BICEPS EXERCISES

The biceps includes a “short head” and a “long head” that work as a single muscle. When the biceps contracts, it pulls the forearm up and rotates it outward.

2. TRICEP EXERCISES

Triceps muscle has three heads. Its major action is extension of the forearm upon the elbow joint, in opposition to the biceps brachii.

3. CHEST EXERCISE

To make sure you work all the chest muscles, include a mix of motions: Press using the flat or incline bench, dumbbells, or seated machine chest press.

4. ABS

Abdominal exercises strength affect the abdominal muscles: rectus abdominis, internal oblique, external oblique, and transversus abdominis.

5. LEGS EXERCISE

The squat is one of the best exercises to tone legs. It also sculpts the butt, hips, and abs.
